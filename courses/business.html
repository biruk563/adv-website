Course Curriculum

A comprehensive learning path designed to develop your business English skills

Module 1: Business English Foundations

Build a strong foundation in business vocabulary and essential communication skills.

  • Business vocabulary and terminology
  • Professional introductions and small talk
  • Phone conversations and scheduling
  • Email etiquette and structure
  • Grammar for business contexts
  • Project: Professional self-introduction and networking
Module 2: Business Writing

Master the art of clear, concise, and effective business writing.

  • Email writing for different purposes
  • Business reports and summaries
  • Proposals and formal documents
  • Writing for digital communication (chat, social media)
  • Proofreading and editing techniques
  • Project: Business proposal and follow-up correspondence
Module 3: Meetings and Discussions

Develop skills for effective participation and leadership in business meetings.

  • Meeting vocabulary and phrases
  • Setting agendas and objectives
  • Expressing opinions and making suggestions
  • Managing disagreements professionally
  • Virtual meeting etiquette and tools
  • Project: Planning and conducting a business meeting
Module 4: Presentations and Public Speaking

Learn to deliver compelling business presentations with confidence.

  • Presentation structure and organization
  • Visual aids and slide design
  • Delivery techniques and body language
  • Handling questions and objections
  • Persuasive speaking strategies
  • Project: Business presentation on a chosen topic
Module 5: Negotiation and Persuasion

Master the language of negotiation and persuasion in business contexts.

  • Negotiation vocabulary and phrases
  • Building rapport and finding common ground
  • Making and responding to proposals
  • Handling objections and difficult situations
  • Closing deals and confirming agreements
  • Project: Role-play negotiation scenario
Module 6: Cross-Cultural Business Communication

Develop awareness and skills for effective communication across different cultures.

  • Cultural dimensions in business
  • Communication styles across cultures
  • Non-verbal communication and etiquette
  • Building international business relationships
  • Avoiding cultural misunderstandings
  • Project: Cross-cultural business case study
Module 7: Job Interviews and Career Development

Prepare for job interviews and career advancement in English-speaking environments.

  • Resume and cover letter writing
  • Interview preparation and common questions
  • Discussing qualifications and experience
  • Salary negotiations
  • Networking for career growth
  • Project: Mock job interview and feedback
Module 8: Capstone Project & Certification

Apply everything you've learned in a comprehensive business simulation project.

  • Integrated business communication project
  • Personal communication style development
  • Final assessment and feedback
  • Certification preparation
  • Ongoing learning resources
  • Project: Complete business communication portfolio

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our business English course

What level of English proficiency do I need to take this course?

We recommend at least an intermediate level of English (B1-B2) to get the most benefit from this course. However, we offer pre-course assessment and can provide additional resources for students who need to strengthen their general English skills before focusing on business communication.

How is this different from general English courses?

Our Business English course is specifically designed for professional contexts. While general English courses focus on everyday communication, our curriculum covers specialized vocabulary, formal writing, presentation skills, negotiation techniques, and cross-cultural business communication that you'll need in a corporate environment.

What is the time commitment for this course?

You should expect to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week to the course. This includes attending live sessions, completing assignments, practicing with language partners, and working on projects. The course is designed to be manageable for working professionals.

Will this course help me prepare for business English exams?

Yes, our curriculum covers many of the skills tested in business English exams such as the Cambridge Business English Certificate (BEC), BULATS, and TOEIC. While we don't specifically teach to these tests, the communication skills you develop will help you perform well on them. We can provide additional resources for specific exam preparation if needed.

How will I practice speaking and conversation skills in an online course?

We use a combination of live video sessions, conversation partners, and recorded speaking assignments to ensure you get plenty of speaking practice. Our interactive sessions include role plays, discussions, and presentations. You'll also be paired with other students for regular conversation practice outside of class time.

Is there any certification upon completion?

Yes, all students who successfully complete the course requirements will receive a certificate of completion from Advanced Language & Computer School. This certificate details the skills you've mastered and can be added to your LinkedIn profile and resume to demonstrate your business English proficiency to employers.
