Course Overview

Everything you need to know about our comprehensive Entrepreneurship program

Launch Your Entrepreneurial Journey

This 10-week course will equip you with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to successfully launch and grow your own business. Whether you're looking to start a tech startup, create a side hustle, or innovate within an existing organization, our curriculum provides a solid foundation across all key entrepreneurial competencies.

Idea Validation & Business Modeling

Learn proven methodologies to generate, validate, and refine business ideas. Develop customer-centric business models that create sustainable value and competitive advantage.

Growth Strategies & Marketing

Master customer acquisition, retention, and growth hacking techniques to scale your business efficiently. Learn how to build and execute effective marketing strategies on limited budgets.

Team Building & Leadership

Develop essential leadership skills to build high-performing teams, create company culture, and navigate the challenges of growing an organization from the ground up.

Funding & Financial Management

Understand different funding options from bootstrapping to venture capital, and learn how to manage finances, create financial projections, and pitch to investors effectively.

Course Curriculum

A comprehensive learning path designed to build your entrepreneurial expertise from the ground up

Module 1: Entrepreneurial Mindset & Opportunity Recognition

Develop the entrepreneurial mindset and learn to identify viable business opportunities.

  • Characteristics of successful entrepreneurs
  • Developing an entrepreneurial mindset
  • Opportunity recognition and evaluation frameworks
  • Market research and trend analysis
  • Problem-solution fit methodology
  • Project: Identify and evaluate three potential business opportunities
Module 2: Business Model Development

Learn to create and validate effective business models that deliver value.

  • Business Model Canvas framework
  • Value proposition design
  • Customer segments and personas
  • Revenue models and pricing strategies
  • Cost structure analysis
  • Project: Develop a complete Business Model Canvas for your venture
Module 3: Customer Discovery & Validation

Master the process of validating your business idea with real customers.

  • Lean Startup methodology
  • Designing effective customer interviews
  • Minimum Viable Product (MVP) development
  • Hypothesis testing and iteration
  • Pivot or persevere decision-making
  • Project: Conduct customer interviews and design an MVP concept
Module 4: Market Strategy & Customer Acquisition

Develop strategies to reach, acquire, and retain customers effectively.

  • Market positioning and competitive analysis
  • Digital marketing fundamentals for startups
  • Growth hacking techniques
  • Customer acquisition channels
  • Metrics and analytics for tracking growth
  • Project: Create a customer acquisition strategy with key metrics
Module 5: Financial Planning & Management

Master the financial aspects of launching and running a startup.

  • Startup financial modeling
  • Cash flow management
  • Unit economics and break-even analysis
  • Financial projections and forecasting
  • Budgeting for startups
  • Project: Develop a financial model for your business concept
Module 6: Funding Strategies & Investor Relations

Explore different funding options and learn how to secure investment.

  • Bootstrapping strategies
  • Friends and family funding
  • Angel investors and venture capital
  • Crowdfunding campaigns
  • Pitch deck development
  • Project: Create a compelling pitch deck for investors
Module 7: Legal Foundations & Intellectual Property

Understand the legal aspects of starting and protecting your business.

  • Business entity selection
  • Intellectual property protection
  • Contracts and agreements
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Risk management
  • Project: Create a legal roadmap for your startup
Module 8: Team Building & Organizational Culture

Learn to build and lead effective teams in a startup environment.

  • Founding team dynamics
  • Hiring strategies for startups
  • Creating company culture and values
  • Leadership in entrepreneurial settings
  • Managing remote and distributed teams
  • Project: Develop a team structure and culture plan
Module 9: Scaling & Growth Management

Prepare for the challenges of scaling your business successfully.

  • Scaling strategies and common pitfalls
  • Operational efficiency and systems
  • Strategic partnerships and alliances
  • International expansion
  • Managing rapid growth
  • Project: Create a scaling roadmap for your business
Module 10: Pitch Day Preparation & Final Project

Refine your business plan and prepare for the culminating pitch day event.

  • Comprehensive business plan development
  • Pitch presentation skills
  • Handling investor questions
  • Demo day preparation
  • Networking with the startup ecosystem
  • Final Project: Present your startup to a panel of investors and entrepreneurs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Entrepreneurship & Startup Fundamentals course

Do I need to have a business idea already to take this course?

No, you don't need to have a specific business idea to start the course. In fact, Module 1 focuses on opportunity recognition and will help you generate and evaluate potential business ideas. However, if you do have an idea you're passionate about, this course will provide the perfect framework to develop and validate it. Many students either come up with new ideas during the course or significantly refine their initial concepts.

Is this course suitable for someone with no business background?

This course is designed to be accessible to people from all backgrounds. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to more advanced concepts. Many of our most successful graduates come from technical, creative, or other non-business backgrounds. In fact, your unique perspective from another field can be a significant advantage in identifying innovative opportunities. The course provides all the business knowledge you'll need in a practical, applicable format.

How much time should I dedicate to this course each week?

We recommend dedicating 8-10 hours per week to get the most out of this course. This includes 2-3 hours of video lessons, 3-4 hours for assignments and projects, and 2-3 hours for practical application and networking. The course is designed to be flexible, so you can adjust your schedule as needed, but consistent engagement will lead to the best results. Many students continue working full-time while taking the course, though it does require dedicated evening and weekend time.

What makes this course different from free online resources?

While there are many free resources available online, this course offers several unique advantages: 1) A structured, comprehensive curriculum that builds progressively, 2) Direct access to experienced entrepreneurs and investors for feedback and mentorship, 3) A supportive community of fellow founders, 4) Hands-on projects with personalized feedback, 5) Accountability and deadlines to keep you moving forward, and 6) Networking opportunities with investors and the startup ecosystem. Our graduates consistently tell us that the structured approach and community aspect were key to their success.

Will I have a complete business plan by the end of the course?

Yes, if you complete all the modules and assignments, you will have all the components of a comprehensive business plan by the end of the course. This includes your validated business model, market analysis, customer acquisition strategy, financial projections, team structure, and scaling plan. More importantly, you'll have validated your core assumptions through customer discovery and developed an MVP concept. The final project brings all these elements together into a cohesive business plan and investor pitch, which you'll present on Pitch Day if you're in the Founder tier.

Can I really start a business in just 10 weeks?

While 10 weeks is not enough time to build a fully scaled business, it is absolutely enough time to validate your idea, develop your business model, create an MVP, and prepare for launch. Many of our students have launched their businesses within 1-3 months of completing the course. The course is designed to accelerate your entrepreneurial journey by providing a proven framework, avoiding common pitfalls, and connecting you with the right resources. Your progress will depend on your commitment level and the complexity of your business idea, but the course provides all the tools you need to get started effectively.
