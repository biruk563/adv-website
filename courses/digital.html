Course Overview

Everything you need to know about our comprehensive Digital Marketing program

Launch Your Digital Marketing Career

This 10-week course will equip you with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to create and execute effective digital marketing campaigns. Whether you're looking to market your own business, advance in your current role, or start a career in digital marketing, our curriculum provides a solid foundation across all key digital channels.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Learn how to optimize websites to rank higher in search engines, drive organic traffic, and implement effective keyword strategies that connect with your target audience.

Paid Advertising

Master the fundamentals of pay-per-click (PPC) advertising across Google, social media platforms, and display networks to create high-converting campaigns with measurable ROI.

Social Media Marketing

Develop strategies for building brand presence and engagement across major social platforms, creating compelling content, and measuring social media campaign effectiveness.

Learn to set up tracking, analyze campaign performance, and make data-informed decisions to continuously improve your marketing efforts and maximize ROI.

Course Curriculum

A comprehensive learning path designed to build your digital marketing expertise from the ground up

Module 1: Digital Marketing Fundamentals

Understand the digital marketing landscape and build a foundation for success.

  • Introduction to digital marketing and its evolution
  • Key digital marketing channels and their roles
  • Understanding the customer journey and sales funnel
  • Creating buyer personas and audience targeting
  • Setting SMART marketing objectives
  • Project: Develop a digital marketing strategy for a business case study
Module 2: Website Optimization & SEO

Learn how to optimize websites for search engines and user experience.

  • SEO fundamentals and search engine algorithms
  • Keyword research and competitive analysis
  • On-page SEO techniques and content optimization
  • Technical SEO and site structure
  • Local SEO strategies for businesses
  • Project: Conduct an SEO audit and create an optimization plan
Module 3: Content Marketing

Master the art of creating valuable content that attracts and engages your target audience.

  • Content marketing strategy and planning
  • Creating different types of content (blogs, videos, infographics, etc.)
  • Content distribution and promotion
  • Storytelling techniques for brands
  • Content calendars and management tools
  • Project: Develop a content marketing plan with sample content pieces
Module 4: Social Media Marketing

Build effective strategies for major social media platforms.

  • Platform-specific strategies (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok)
  • Creating engaging social media content
  • Community management and engagement
  • Social media scheduling and management tools
  • Measuring social media performance
  • Project: Create a social media campaign for a specific platform
Module 5: Pay-Per-Click Advertising

Learn to create and manage effective paid advertising campaigns.

  • Google Ads fundamentals (Search, Display, Video)
  • Social media advertising (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
  • Campaign structure and targeting options
  • Ad copywriting and creative best practices
  • Bid management and budget optimization
  • Project: Set up and optimize a Google Ads campaign
Module 6: Email Marketing

Develop effective email marketing campaigns that convert.

  • Email marketing strategy and list building
  • Email design and copywriting
  • Automation and drip campaigns
  • A/B testing and optimization
  • Deliverability and compliance (GDPR, CAN-SPAM)
  • Project: Create an email marketing sequence for a customer journey
Module 7: Analytics & Data-Driven Marketing

Learn to measure, analyze, and optimize your marketing efforts.

  • Google Analytics setup and configuration
  • Key performance indicators (KPIs) for different channels
  • Creating custom reports and dashboards
  • Conversion tracking and attribution models
  • Data analysis for marketing optimization
  • Project: Set up analytics tracking and create a performance dashboard
Module 8: Conversion Rate Optimization

Optimize your digital assets to increase conversions and ROI.

  • CRO principles and methodology
  • User experience (UX) and landing page optimization
  • A/B testing and experimentation
  • Persuasive copywriting techniques
  • Tools for CRO and testing
  • Project: Conduct a CRO audit and create an optimization plan
Module 9: Mobile Marketing

Develop strategies for the mobile-first digital world.

  • Mobile marketing fundamentals and trends
  • Mobile SEO and responsive design
  • App store optimization (ASO)
  • SMS and push notification marketing
  • Location-based marketing strategies
  • Project: Create a mobile marketing strategy for a business
Module 10: Digital Marketing Strategy & Final Project

Bring together all aspects of digital marketing into a comprehensive strategy.

  • Integrated digital marketing planning
  • Budget allocation and resource planning
  • Marketing technology stack and tools
  • Measuring marketing ROI
  • Future trends in digital marketing
  • Final Project: Develop a comprehensive digital marketing plan for a real business

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Digital Marketing Fundamentals course

Do I need prior marketing experience to take this course?

No, this course is designed to accommodate beginners with no prior marketing experience. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to more advanced concepts. However, if you do have some marketing background, you'll still benefit from the digital-specific strategies, tools, and hands-on projects that will help you apply your knowledge in the digital realm.

Will I need to purchase any additional tools or software?

Most of the tools we use in the course offer free versions or trials that are sufficient for learning purposes. These include Google Analytics, Google Ads (with test accounts), social media platforms, and various free SEO tools. We provide a list of recommended tools before the course starts, but purchasing premium versions is optional and not required to complete the course successfully.

How hands-on is this course?

This course is extremely hands-on. Each module includes practical exercises and projects that apply the concepts you've learned. You'll create real campaigns, develop marketing strategies, analyze data, and optimize based on results. By the end of the course, you'll have a portfolio of projects that demonstrate your skills to potential employers or clients. We believe in learning by doing, not just theory.

Is the course content updated regularly?

Yes, digital marketing is a rapidly evolving field, and our curriculum is updated quarterly to reflect the latest platform changes, algorithm updates, and industry best practices. When you enroll, you get lifetime access to the course materials, including all future updates. Our instructors are active practitioners who bring real-world, current examples to their teaching.

How much time should I dedicate to this course each week?

We recommend dedicating 8-10 hours per week to get the most out of this course. This includes 2-3 hours of video lessons, 2-3 hours for assignments and projects, and 2-4 hours for practice and experimentation with the tools and platforms. The course is designed to be flexible, so you can adjust your schedule as needed, but consistent engagement will lead to the best results.

Will this course help me get a job in digital marketing?

Yes, this course is designed with employability in mind. The curriculum covers the most in-demand digital marketing skills that employers are looking for. The Professional plan includes job placement assistance, resume reviews, and interview preparation. Additionally, the portfolio of projects you'll build throughout the course will demonstrate your capabilities to potential employers. Many of our graduates have successfully transitioned into digital marketing roles or advanced in their current positions.
